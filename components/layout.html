Layout Size

Easily make an element as wide or as tall (relative to its parent) with our width and height utilities. Includes support for 25% , 50% , 75% , and 100% by default. The classes are named using the format {property}-{size} for xs and {property}-{breakpoint}-{size} for sm, md, lg, and xl.
